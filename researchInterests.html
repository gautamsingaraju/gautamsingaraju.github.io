Research Interests

My research focus is towards designing and creating tools for Secure System Administration to deal with distributed systems and services (such as email, DNS). My research brings the concepts of network security and distributed systems to System administration to design reliable, secure and deployable systems. My experience as a System Administrator for a global non-profit organization helped in understanding the requirements for system administrators.
