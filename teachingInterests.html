Teaching Experience

I have actively designed lab infrastructure and the lab classes for ITIS 3100: Introduction to IT, an undergraduate course which now runs into two sections in the department. The class material that I designed is a part of an NSF grant material focused on that develops faculty workshops where the faculty members from different universities learn mechanisms to incorporate cyber games and interactive simulations into their classes. I have been nominated for the outstanding TA award. I presented my teaching experiences using virtualization to teach system administration at System Education Workshop at USENIX LISA 2007.
